OUR LEGACY


Samay Watch Company is a third generation family run company retailing luxury watches. With its inception in 1967,
Samay Watch Company was one of the initial players to enter the luxury watch retail business in India.


The company was founded by Mr.lorem , who was a watchmaker himself. With his sons and grandsons carrying on the family legacy,
Samay Watch Company has successfully established itself as the leader in the business of luxury Swiss watches in India.


Today, our legacy entails a strong retail presence as an authorized retailer for Swiss watches, and one of the most extensive client networks in India. We currently have lot of showrooms and most of
luxury brands in our portfolio, and a state-of-the-art service center which services not just those, but the entire gamut of luxury timepieces.
