Qus: What is Character at index 15 in ’30 Days of JavaScript’ string? Use charAt() method.
Ans:
<script>
const anyString = "30 Days of JavaScript";


console.log(
`Character at index 15 in ’30 Days of JavaScript’ string is '${anyString.charAt(15)}'` );

</script>

Output
Character at index 15 in ’30 Days of JavaScript’ string is 'S'
