Qus: What is the rule set?
Ans:
  • Each (valid) declaration block is preceded by one or more comma-separated selectors, which are conditions selecting some elements of the page.
  • A selector list and an associated declarations block, together, are called a ruleset, or often a rule.

Example

        div {
            background-color: darkslategrey;
            border: 2px solid darkgoldenrod;
        }
  • in this example two properties are used which are background-color & border and their's values are darkslategrey & 2px, solid, darkgoldenrod. and ";" is used for declarations block, together, are called a ruleset, or often a rule.
